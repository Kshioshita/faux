Welcome!

Hi! I'm a Kindness Bag! I might have been a gift or you might want to give me to someone you care about. My purpose is to share kindness and pass it on to someone else. Fill me with a surprise, an encouraging note, a memory, etc. If you can put a smile on someone's face, then I've done my job.
