Welcome!

Hi! I'm a Kindness Bag! I might be gift someone gave you or you might want to give me to someone you care about. It's purpose is to share kindness and pass it on to someone else. Fill me with a surprise, an encouraging note, a memory, etc. If you can put a smile on someone's face, I've done my job.
